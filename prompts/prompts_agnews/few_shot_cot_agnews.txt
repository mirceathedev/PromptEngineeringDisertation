Label meanings:

[WORLD]
Politics, war, diplomacy, governments, elections, countries, international relations, disasters, courts, public security, hostage crises, or global public affairs.

[SPORTS]
Teams, athletes, games, tournaments, scores, matches, coaches, leagues, player status, injuries, arenas, or sporting events.

[BUSINESS]
Economy, finance, companies, markets, stocks, earnings, trade, mergers, banking, jobs, ownership, lawsuits, refunds, profits, sales, or business forecasts.

[SCI / TECH]
Science, technology, software, hardware, internet, search engines, AI, cybersecurity, telecom, gadgets, space, medicine, research, viruses, malware, or technical innovation.

Critical rules:
- Choose the MAIN topic, not isolated words.
- Do NOT choose [WORLD] just because the text is news.
- Do NOT choose [BUSINESS] just because a company is mentioned.
- Do NOT choose [SCI / TECH] just because a technology company is mentioned.
- A sports team sale can still be [SPORTS] if the article is mainly about the team as a sports entity.
- A technology company lawsuit or earnings story is [BUSINESS] if the main topic is financial/legal activity.
- A software, search, virus, hardware, medicine, science, or research story is [SCI / TECH] if the main topic is the technology itself.
