Intent meanings:

[BALANCE NOT UPDATED AFTER BANK TRANSFER]
The customer made or received a bank transfer, but their own account balance has not updated.

[BALANCE NOT UPDATED AFTER CHEQUE OR CASH DEPOSIT]
The customer deposited cash or a cheque, but their own balance has not updated.

[CARD PAYMENT FEE CHARGED]
The customer was charged a fee for paying by card.

[CASH WITHDRAWAL CHARGE]
The customer was charged a fee for withdrawing cash.

[DECLINED CASH WITHDRAWAL]
The customer tried to withdraw cash, but the withdrawal was declined, rejected, or failed.

[DIRECT DEBIT PAYMENT NOT RECOGNISED]
The customer sees a direct debit payment they do not recognize.

[TRANSACTION CHARGED TWICE]
The same transaction, purchase, or payment appears twice.

[TRANSFER FEE CHARGED]
The customer was charged a fee for sending or receiving a bank transfer.

[TRANSFER NOT RECEIVED BY RECIPIENT]
The customer sent a transfer, but the recipient has not received it.

[WRONG AMOUNT OF CASH RECEIVED]
The customer withdrew cash but received the wrong amount from the ATM or cash machine.

Important boundaries:
- Balance not updated = the customer’s own account balance is wrong or delayed.
- Transfer not received = the other person or recipient did not get the money.
- Transfer fee = fee for a bank transfer.
- Card payment fee = fee for paying by card.
- Cash withdrawal charge = fee for taking cash out.
- Declined cash withdrawal = ATM/cash withdrawal did not go through.
- Wrong amount of cash received = ATM gave too much or too little cash.
- Charged twice = duplicate transaction.
- Direct debit not recognised = unknown automatic/direct debit payment.
