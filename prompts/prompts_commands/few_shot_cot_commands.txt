Intent meanings:

[BALANCE NOT UPDATED AFTER BANK TRANSFER]
The customer made or received a bank transfer, but their own account balance has not updated.

[BALANCE NOT UPDATED AFTER CHEQUE OR CASH DEPOSIT]
The customer deposited cash or a cheque, but their own account balance has not updated.

[CARD PAYMENT FEE CHARGED]
The customer was charged a fee for paying by card.

[CASH WITHDRAWAL CHARGE]
The customer was charged a fee for withdrawing cash.

[DECLINED CASH WITHDRAWAL]
The customer tried to withdraw cash, but the withdrawal was declined, rejected, frozen, denied, or failed.

[DIRECT DEBIT PAYMENT NOT RECOGNISED]
The customer sees a direct debit payment, debit payment, or unknown charge they do not recognize.

[TRANSACTION CHARGED TWICE]
The same transaction, purchase, payment, or charge appears twice.

[TRANSFER FEE CHARGED]
The customer was charged a fee for sending or receiving a bank transfer.

[TRANSFER NOT RECEIVED BY RECIPIENT]
The customer sent a transfer, but the recipient, friend, landlord, receiver, payee, or other person has not received it.

[WRONG AMOUNT OF CASH RECEIVED]
The customer withdrew cash but received the wrong amount from the ATM or cash machine.

Critical distinctions:
- Own balance not updated after transfer → [BALANCE NOT UPDATED AFTER BANK TRANSFER]
- Recipient / friend / landlord / payee did not get the money → [TRANSFER NOT RECEIVED BY RECIPIENT]
- Cash or cheque deposit missing from own balance → [BALANCE NOT UPDATED AFTER CHEQUE OR CASH DEPOSIT]
- Fee for card payment, payment, purchase, or paying by card → [CARD PAYMENT FEE CHARGED]
- Fee for bank transfer or sending money → [TRANSFER FEE CHARGED]
- Fee for ATM or cash withdrawal → [CASH WITHDRAWAL CHARGE]
- Cash withdrawal failed, was declined, rejected, denied, frozen, or did not work → [DECLINED CASH WITHDRAWAL]
- ATM gave too much, too little, less than requested, or wrong cash amount → [WRONG AMOUNT OF CASH RECEIVED]
- Same transaction appears twice → [TRANSACTION CHARGED TWICE]
- Unknown direct debit, debit payment, or payment the user did not make → [DIRECT DEBIT PAYMENT NOT RECOGNISED]
