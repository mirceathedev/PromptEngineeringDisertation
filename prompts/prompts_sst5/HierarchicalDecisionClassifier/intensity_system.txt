CORE INTENSITY PRINCIPLE
────────────────────────────────────────

Only decide strength.

Intensity is not only about extreme words.

A review can be [VERY] because of:

- one-sided strong criticism or praise

Do NOT require words like “masterpiece”, “brilliant”, “awful”, or “terrible” for [VERY].

If the review is mixed, limited, restrained, or balanced by a caveat, usually choose [NORMAL].

If the review is broad, total, one-sided, forceful, or dismissive, usually choose [VERY].
